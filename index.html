Core Features

Built for nomads who value community and safety

01

Convoy Feed

Post your travel routes, find others heading the same direction, and join live journeys. See who's traveling from Mumbai to Goa this week.

02

Connect (Dating)

Swipe-based matching for nomads. Filter by travel pace (Fast/Slow/Steady), mode (Solo/Couple), and style (Off-grid/Campgrounds). Find partners who move like you.

03

Campfire (Community)

Join circles, share stories, ask questions, and attend meetups. Private group chats, wiki resources, and Q&A forums for the nomad community.

04

Services Directory

Find verified mechanics, electricians, solar techs, and carpenters who understand custom van builds. Location-based helper discovery with direct messaging.

05

Trust-First Safety

Strict 5-step verification with rig photo proof. Community promise keeps creeps and bots out. Verified badges for trusted members and professionals.

06

Stories & Messaging

24-hour expiring stories, private DMs, group circles, and real-time chat. Share your journey and stay connected with your nomad tribe.
